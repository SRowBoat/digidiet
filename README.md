# digidiet
Digital Diet and Footprint Calculator


# Digital Footprint Energy Index

> **The Conversion Rate:**
> **1 Point** = Roughly **0.1 kWh** of data center electricity + **0.25 liters** of cooling fluid.

---

## 📊 Energy Consumption Framework

### 1. Video & Streaming (Per Hour)
| Activity | Energy Cost |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Audio Only** (Spotify, Apple Music) | `1 Point` |
| **Standard Definition Video** (TikTok, YouTube 480p) | `3 Points` |
| **High-Definition Video** (Netflix, YouTube 1080p) | `10 Points` |
| **4K Ultra-HD Video** (Smart TV streaming) | `30 Points` |

### 2. Communication & Feeds (Per Hour)
| Activity | Energy Cost |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Static Text/Reading** (Substack, Wikipedia) | `0.1 Points` |
| **Text Chatting** (WhatsApp, iMessage) | `0.5 Points` *(Flat Daily Rate)* |
| **Social Scrolling** (Algorithm-driven feeds) | `8 Points` |

### 3. Generative AI (Per Prompt)
| Activity | Energy Cost |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Text Query** (Short LLM answers) | `0.2 Points` |
| **Deep Reasoning / Multi-Turn Chat** (Coding, long research) | `1 Point` |
| **Image/Video Generation** (Midjourney, DALL-E) | `5 Points` |

### 4. Background Services (Flat Daily Rate)
| Activity | Energy Cost |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Smart Home Baseline** (Ring cameras, smart plugs idle) | `2 Points` |
| **Automated Cloud Backups** (Continuous photo/4K video sync) | `15 Points` |

---

## 🎯 Where Do You Score?

Calculate your average daily points, multiply by **365**, and see where you land:

* **Tier 1: Digital Ghost** — `0 – 500 Points/Year`
* **Tier 2: Lean Consumer** — `501 – 15,000 Points/Year`
* **Tier 3: Cloud Citizen** — `15,001 – 60,000 Points/Year`
* **Tier 4: Hyper-Saturated** — `60,001+ Points/Year`

---

## ⚡ The Energy Disconnect: Real-World Costs

If an individual user hits an annual score of **30,000 points** (an active *Cloud Citizen*), they are pulling roughly **3,000 kWh** of background electricity per year.

### Financial Breakdown
* **What the Data Center pays for that energy:** `~$120.00`
* **What it would cost on a Standard US Home Bill:** `~$480.00`
* **What it would cost on a European/UK Home Bill:** `~$1,200.00`

> 🚗 **The Bottom Line:** A heavy digital footprint pulls enough raw energy to match driving a traditional compact car 20 km to work every single day for a year.
